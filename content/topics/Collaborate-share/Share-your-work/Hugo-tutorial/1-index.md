## Welcome to the Hugo Tutorial

In this step-by-step tutorial, you'll learn how to:

- Install __**Hugo Extended**__ on your platform
- Create and configure a Hugo site
- Run a local development server
- Deploy your site to __**GitHub Pages**__ (bonus)
- Add content to your Hugo site using Markdown and HTML

### Choose Your Installation Platform

Please choose your operating system to begin installation:

- [Install on Windows](/topics/Collaborate-share/Share-you-work/Hugo-tutorial/2-install-windows)
- [Install on Linux](/topics/Collaborate-share/Share-you-work/Hugo-tutorial/3-install-linux)

Once installed, you may continue to the [Using Hugo Guide](/topics/Collaborate-share/Share-you-work/Hugo-tutorial/4-launch-site/) for building and deploying your site.
